# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name

**AI-Powered Fashion Discovery Browser Extension**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. What to Build

Build a browser extension that allows users to identify and shop for
clothing and accessories seen in online videos.

When a user pauses a video on supported platforms (such as YouTube,
Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Instagram, TikTok, and similar services),
they can activate the extension. The extension captures the current
video frame, identifies the clothing and accessories visible in the
scene, and returns either the exact products or visually similar
alternatives from multiple fashion retailers.

The experience should be fast, intuitive, and minimally disruptive,
allowing users to continue watching their content while browsing
recommendations.

The long-term vision is to make every video instantly shoppable by
transforming visual inspiration into a seamless shopping experience.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Targeted Users

## Primary Users

-   Fashion enthusiasts looking to recreate outfits from videos.
-   Online shoppers who prefer visual discovery over text-based search.
-   Users who frequently watch content on streaming platforms and social
    media.
-   People inspired by celebrities, influencers, and creators.

## Secondary Users

-   Content creators and influencers.
-   Stylists and fashion consultants.
-   Costume designers.
-   Students and young professionals seeking affordable outfit
    alternatives.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Features

## Core Features (MVP)

-   Capture the current paused video frame.
-   Detect multiple clothing items and accessories independently.
-   Identify exact or visually similar fashion products.
-   Display product recommendations from multiple retailers.
-   Show product details:
    -   Product image
    -   Product title
    -   Brand
    -   Price
    -   Purchase link
-   Browse results without interrupting video playback.

## Search & Discovery

-   Similarity-based recommendations.
-   Multiple alternatives for each detected item.
-   Budget-friendly alternatives.
-   Premium alternatives.
-   Multiple retailer support.
